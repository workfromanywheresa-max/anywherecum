Donation Guidelines

1. General Information

  • Donations are completely optional and greatly appreciated.
  • Please ensure all payment details are entered correctly.
  • All contributions are voluntary.

2. Crypto Donations

  • Crypto donations are final once sent on the blockchain.
  • Always double-check wallet address and network before sending.
  • USDT (TRC20), BNB (BEP20), and ETH networks are supported.
  • Sending to the wrong network may result in loss of funds.

3. PayPal Donations

  • Please ensure correct details before sending your donation.
  • Chargebacks or disputes are handled according to PayPal policies.

4. Card Donations

  • Card donations are processed securely through Yoco.
  • Please ensure your payment details are correct before submitting.

5. Fair Use

  • All donations should be genuine and intentional.
  • We may review unusual activity to maintain fairness.

6. Important Note

💛 Donations are voluntary contributions made to support the platform. Thank you for your kindness and support.

7. Thank You

Your support is truly appreciated, no matter the amount.
