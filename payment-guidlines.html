Payment Guidelines

Access Rules

🔒 For security reasons, VIP Access is limited to one active device at a time. Logging in on a new device or browser will automatically log out the previous session.

1. General Rules

  • All payments must be made using correct details provided.
  • Users must ensure all information entered is accurate.
  • Orders are only processed after successful payment confirmation.

2. Crypto Payments

  • All crypto transactions are final and non-refundable.
  • Send only to the correct wallet address and network.
  • USDT must use TRC20 only, BNB BEP20 only, ETH network only.
  • Wrong network or address may result in permanent loss of funds.
  • Blockchain confirmation is required before processing.

3. PayPal Payments

  • Payments may be subject to PayPal processing delays.
  • Chargebacks or disputes may result in account restrictions.
  • Ensure correct payment details before sending.
  • Refunds are handled according to service policy.

4. PayFast / Card Payments

  • Card payments are processed securely via PayFast.
  • Some transactions may require verification.
  • Failed payments may not be retried automatically.
  • Processing depends on bank speed.

5. Fraud & Misuse

  • Fake or fraudulent payment details are not allowed.
  • We reserve the right to cancel suspicious transactions.
  • Abuse of the system may result in a permanent block.

6. Processing Time

  • Crypto: 5–15 minutes after confirmation.
  • PayFast / Card: Usually instant to a few minutes.
  • PayPal: 5–15 minutes processing time.
  • Delays beyond 15 minutes should be reported.
⚡ Payments are usually processed within 5–15 minutes depending on network or bank delays.

7. Agreement

By making a payment, you agree to these rules.
